# Privacy Policy for MedReminder

**Effective Date:** March 18, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the **MedReminder** mobile application (the "Application") for mobile devices.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The Application operates entirely offline and **does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, health information, or analytics to external servers**.

All data entered into the Application, including but not limited to:
*   Medicine names
*   Dosage schedules and frequencies
*   Medication intake history (logs)

...is stored completely locally and securely on your device using local device storage.

## 2. Permissions Required
To function properly, the Application requests the following device permissions:
*   **Notifications (and Alarms & Reminders):** The Application requires this permission solely to schedule and display local, offline alerts when it is time to take your medication. No scheduling data or notification history is transmitted off your device.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Since the Application does not transmit data to external servers, your data is never shared with, sold to, or accessed by third parties.

## 4. Data Security
The security of your data is ensured by the operating system of your device. We recommend keeping your device protected with a passcode, face recognition, or fingerprint lock to secure access to the Application.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
